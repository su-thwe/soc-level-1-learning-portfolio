## 1. Objective

This room introduced the daily responsibilities of a Junior Security Analyst, also known as a SOC Level 1 Analyst. The main purpose was to understand how a beginner analyst supports a Security Operations Center by reviewing alerts, investigating suspicious activity, documenting findings, and escalating issues when needed.

## 2. Key Concepts Learned

- A SOC is a team responsible for monitoring, detecting, and responding to security threats.
- A Junior SOC Analyst is usually the first person to review security alerts (on the frontline of defense).
- SOC work involves teamwork, communication, and continuous learning.
- Not every alert is a confirmed incident; analysts need to triage and prioritize.
- Escalation is important when an alert is too complex or serious for Level 1.

## 3. SOC Analyst Responsibilities

A Junior SOC Analyst may be responsible for:

- Monitoring security alerts
- Investigating suspicious activity
- Reviewing tickets
- Collecting basic evidence
- Communicating with teammates
- Escalating complex incidents
- Learning new threats and defensive techniques

## 4. SOC Team Members

| Role | What They Do | Why It Matters |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Junior SOC Analyst / L1 Analyst | Reviews and triages alerts | First line of defense |
| Senior Analyst / L2 Analyst | Handles deeper investigations | Supports L1 analysts with complex cases |
| SOC Engineer | Maintains security tools and detections | Makes sure alerts and tools work properly |
| SOC Manager | Oversees the SOC team | Manages operations and reporting |
| Incident Responder | Handles major incidents | Responds when serious security events happen |

## 5. My Main Takeaway

The biggest thing I learned is that SOC work is not done alone. A Junior SOC Analyst works as part of a team by reviewing alerts, collecting evidence, communicating findings, and escalating issues when needed. Though the role is beginner-friendly, I am surprised to learn how often the first line of defense against cyber incidents are SOC analysts.
